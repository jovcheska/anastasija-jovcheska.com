Macedonia Marekting Map

An online platform that aims to connect marketers and marketing agencies
with marketing service seekers.

Homepage

Problem

  • Many marketing agencies / marketers find it difficult to reach customers due to the new pandemic situation
  • It is a challenge for clients to find an agency/ marketer that will offer/possess the exact specifications required by the client

Solution

  • Through the new platform, marketers will have more customers and more regular income
  • Marketers will be able to work on projects according to the skills they know best
  • On the new platform customers will be able to find a suitable candidate according to the desired specifications and budget

How does it work?

Marketers and clients fill out a questionnaire in which they provide information about their specifications, after which Brainster manually makes a connection between the client and the candidate main points to which we are referring.

After completing the customer questionnaire, Brainster will contact each of them with suggestions for suitable marketers

After completing the Marketer Questionnaire, Brainster will contact the appropriate candidates for more information and they will be listed on the design map

The marketing map page will list all marketers / agencies available to hire
